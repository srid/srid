[Azufre 10%](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00371PJ1E)

## Instructions

Put soap in hot water for about a minute, then apply it on face & scalp. Let soak for no less than 5 minutes. Rinse. Do it every day. Suitable oily and/or inflammatory skin.

The soap can cause dryness. Apply a moisturizer (e.g.: Eucerin Urea 5% moisturizer) on face & scalp after washing.
